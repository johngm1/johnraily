Why is Biodiversity Crucial?

💧

Ecological Balance

It maintains the health and stability of ecosystems, providing clean water, air, and fertile soil.

💊

Resource Hub

Earth's organisms are the natural source of food, medicine, and industrial materials, supporting human life and development.

🌎

Climate Regulation

Forest and marine ecosystems absorb large amounts of carbon dioxide, helping to regulate global climate change.
